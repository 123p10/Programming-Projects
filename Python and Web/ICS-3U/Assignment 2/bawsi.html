About BAWSI

The Bay Area Women's Sports Initiative (BAWSI) is a public benefit, nonprofit corporation with a mission to create programs and partnerships throug which women athletes bring health hope and wholeness to our community. Founded in 2005 by Olympic and World Cup Soccer stars Brand Chastain and Julie Foudy and Marle Bjornsrud, former general manager of the San Jose CyberRays women's professional soccer team, BAWSI provides a meaningful path for women athletes to become a more visible and valued part of the Bay Area sports culture.
